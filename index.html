Welcome to Two Friends - After School Child Care Centre

Nestled in the heart of Nugegoda, Sri Lanka, Two Friends - After School Child Care Centre is more than just a daycare; it's a vibrant community where young minds blossom. We offer a unique blend of educational support, playful activities, and nurturing care, tailored to each child's needs.

Our centre is a safe haven where children can explore, learn, and grow after school hours. We believe in fostering a love for learning through a mix of fun and academic guidance. Our dedicated staff provides personalized attention, ensuring that your child's cognitive, social, and emotional development is nurtured every step of the way.

From engaging homework support to creative playtime, each day at Two Friends is an adventure. We're not just caring for your children; we're helping them build lasting friendships, discover new interests, and develop their potential in a warm, welcoming environment.

Join our Two Friends family and give your child the gift of a joyful learning journey!

Our Services

Comprehensive Daycare

Our daycare service is more than just supervision; it's a home away from home where your child can relax, play, and learn. We offer a variety of activities that cater to different interests, including arts and crafts, storytelling, and outdoor play. Our facilities are equipped with everything needed to ensure your child's comfort and safety.

Homework Support

Understanding the importance of academic success, we provide dedicated homework support to help your child excel. Our experienced staff assists with assignments, reinforces learning concepts, and encourages effective study habits. We strive to make learning an enjoyable experience, fostering a positive attitude towards education.

School Pick-up Service

To ease your daily routine, we offer a reliable school pick-up service. Our staff ensures a safe and timely pick-up from school, providing peace of mind for parents. Once at our centre, children can unwind, have a snack, and then transition into our after-school program.
